Emerging Technologies
KGSP Summer Program

Welcome to the 2026 KGSP Emerging Technologies Program.
Here you can find all of the materials, labs, and resources across eight weeks.

PROGRAM SCHEDULE

Weekly Breakdown

Week 1: June 21 – 25

Data Science & Artificial Intelligence — Part 1
An introduction to data science and the foundations of artificial intelligence.

Week 2: June 28 – July 2

Data Science & Artificial Intelligence — Part 2
Building on the fundamentals of data science and modern AI.

Week 3: July 5 – 9

Cybersecurity & Blockchain Technology — Part 1
Security principles and the fundamentals of blockchain technology.

Week 4: July 12 – 16

Cybersecurity & Blockchain Technology — Part 2
A deeper exploration of cybersecurity and decentralized systems.

Week 5: July 19 – 23

Introduction to Quantum Computing
The core ideas behind quantum computing and how it differs from classical computing.

Week 6: July 26 – 30

Principles of Cloud and Edge Computing
How modern applications run across cloud data centers and edge devices.

Week 7: August 2 – 6

Internet of Things (IoT)
Connecting the physical world through sensors, devices, and IoT networks.

Week 8: August 9 – 13

Robotics, Drones & Autonomous Systems
Robotics, drones, and the systems that let machines sense and act autonomously.
